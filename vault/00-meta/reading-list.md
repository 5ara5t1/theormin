# Reading list

Canonical version: [`data/books.yaml`](../../data/books.yaml). This page is a human-readable mirror.

## Phase 1 — Mathematical foundation

### Real analysis

- Tao, *Analysis I* — primary, in progress
- Zorich, *Mathematical Analysis* vol 1 — primary, in progress
- Demidovich, *Problems in Mathematical Analysis* — ~500 problem target

### Linear algebra

- Strang, *Introduction to Linear Algebra* — first pass, in progress. Pair with MIT 18.06 lectures.
- Shilov, *Linear Algebra* — second pass
- Axler, *Linear Algebra Done Right* — third pass

### ODE

- Arnold, *Ordinary Differential Equations* — primary
- Hirsch-Smale-Devaney, *Dynamical Systems* — scaffolding for Arnold
- Krasnov, Kiselev, Makarenko, *Problems in ODEs*

### Complex analysis

- Markushevich vol 1
- Needham, *Visual Complex Analysis*

### Calculus of variations

- Gelfand & Fomin, *Calculus of Variations*

## Phase 2 — Mechanics and fields

- Vladimirov, *Equations of Mathematical Physics* (PDE)
- Frankel, *Geometry of Physics* (tensor calc + diff geom)
- Hassani, *Mathematical Physics* (reference)
- Sivukhin, *General Course of Physics* (5 vols)
- Irodov, *Problems in General Physics* — ~2,000 target
- Landau-Lifshitz vol 1 (Mechanics)
- Landau-Lifshitz vol 2 (Classical Theory of Fields)
- Goldstein, *Classical Mechanics*

## Phase 3 — Quantum mechanics

- Kolmogorov-Fomin (functional analysis, Soviet first pass)
- Hall, *Quantum Theory for Mathematicians* (bridge to QM)
- Georgi, *Lie Algebras in Particle Physics*
- Landau-Lifshitz vol 3 (QM)
- Shankar, *Principles of Quantum Mechanics*
- Sakurai, *Modern Quantum Mechanics*
- Galitski et al., *Exploring Quantum Mechanics* — ~700 target

## Phase 4 — Advanced theoretical physics

- Shiryaev, *Probability*
- Bender & Orszag, *Advanced Mathematical Methods*
- Trefethen & Bau, *Numerical Linear Algebra*
- Landau-Lifshitz vol 4 (QED)
- Landau-Lifshitz vol 5 (Stat Phys 1)
- Landau-Lifshitz vol 6 (Fluid Mechanics)
- Peskin & Schroeder, *An Introduction to QFT*
- Kardar, *Statistical Physics of Particles* and *of Fields*
- Zinn-Justin, *Quantum Field Theory and Critical Phenomena*

## Phase 5 — Continuous media + kinetics

- Landau-Lifshitz vol 7 (Elasticity)
- Landau-Lifshitz vol 8 (Electrodynamics of Continuous Media)
- Landau-Lifshitz vol 9 (Stat Phys 2)
- Landau-Lifshitz vol 10 (Physical Kinetics)
- Hatcher, *Algebraic Topology* (optional)

## Phase 6 — Modern theoretical physics

### QFT

- Tong, *Lectures on Quantum Field Theory* (free, entry point)
- Schwartz, *Quantum Field Theory and the Standard Model* (primary)
- Srednicki, *Quantum Field Theory* (free alternative)
- Weinberg vols 1–3 (depth reference)
- Faddeev & Slavnov, *Gauge Fields*
- Polyakov, *Gauge Fields and Strings*

### GR

- Hartle, *Gravity* (intro)
- Carroll, *Spacetime and Geometry* (primary)
- Wald, *General Relativity* (follow-on)
- MTW, *Gravitation* (reference)

### EFT

- Manohar, *Effective Field Theories* (lecture notes)
- Cohen, TASI 2018 lectures
- Burgess, *Introduction to Effective Field Theory*

### Condensed matter

- Altland & Simons, *Condensed Matter Field Theory*
- Coleman, *Introduction to Many-Body Physics*
