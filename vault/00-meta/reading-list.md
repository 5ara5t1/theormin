# Reading list

The canonical version is [`data/books.yaml`](../../data/books.yaml) at the repo root — that file feeds the README dashboard. This page is a human-readable mirror.

## Phase 1 — Mathematical foundation

### Real analysis

- **Tao**, *Analysis I* — primary, gentle proof builder, in progress
- **Zorich**, *Mathematical Analysis* vol 1 — primary, Soviet rigor, in progress
- **Demidovich**, *Problems in Mathematical Analysis* — problem volume, ~500 target

### Linear algebra

- **Strang**, *Introduction to Linear Algebra* — primary first pass, geometric, in progress. Pair with MIT 18.06 lectures.
- **Shilov**, *Linear Algebra* — Soviet depth, second pass
- **Axler**, *Linear Algebra Done Right* — modern axiomatic, third pass

### ODE

- **Arnold**, *Ordinary Differential Equations* — primary, geometric, demanding
- **Hirsch-Smale-Devaney**, *Dynamical Systems* — scaffolding for Arnold, read in parallel
- **Krasnov, Kiselev, Makarenko**, *Problems in ODEs* — problem volume

### Complex analysis

- **Markushevich** vol 1 — Soviet canonical
- **Needham**, *Visual Complex Analysis* — geometric complement

### Calculus of variations

- **Gelfand & Fomin**, *Calculus of Variations* — short, beautiful, only book needed

## Phase 2 — Mechanics and fields

- **Vladimirov**, *Equations of Mathematical Physics* — PDE
- **Frankel**, *Geometry of Physics* — tensor calculus + intro differential geometry
- **Hassani**, *Mathematical Physics* — reference for special functions, integral transforms, etc.
- **Sivukhin**, *General Course of Physics* (5 vols) — general physics review
- **Irodov**, *Problems in General Physics* — ~2,000 target
- **Landau-Lifshitz vol 1** (Mechanics) — primary
- **Landau-Lifshitz vol 2** (Classical Theory of Fields) — primary
- **Goldstein**, *Classical Mechanics* — Western complement

## Phase 3 — Quantum mechanics

- **Kolmogorov-Fomin** — functional analysis Soviet first pass
- **Hall**, *Quantum Theory for Mathematicians* — bridge from functional analysis to QM
- **Georgi**, *Lie Algebras in Particle Physics* — group theory
- **Landau-Lifshitz vol 3** (QM) — primary
- **Shankar**, *Principles of Quantum Mechanics* — clearest Western text
- **Sakurai**, *Modern Quantum Mechanics* — Western complement
- **Galitski et al.**, *Exploring Quantum Mechanics* — ~700 problem target

## Phase 4 — Advanced theoretical physics

- **Shiryaev**, *Probability*
- **Bender & Orszag**, *Advanced Mathematical Methods* — asymptotics
- **Trefethen & Bau**, *Numerical Linear Algebra*
- **Landau-Lifshitz vol 4** (QED)
- **Landau-Lifshitz vol 5** (Stat Phys 1)
- **Landau-Lifshitz vol 6** (Fluid Mechanics)
- **Peskin & Schroeder** — Western QFT complement
- **Kardar**, *Statistical Physics of Particles* and *of Fields* — modern stat mech
- **Zinn-Justin**, *Quantum Field Theory and Critical Phenomena* — stat mech ↔ QFT bridge

## Phase 5 — Continuous media + kinetics

- **Landau-Lifshitz vol 7** (Elasticity)
- **Landau-Lifshitz vol 8** (Electrodynamics of Continuous Media)
- **Landau-Lifshitz vol 9** (Stat Phys 2)
- **Landau-Lifshitz vol 10** (Physical Kinetics)
- **Hatcher**, *Algebraic Topology* — optional, for field-theory / TQFT tracks

## Phase 6 — Modern theoretical physics

### QFT

- **Tong**, *Lectures on Quantum Field Theory* — free entry point
- **Schwartz**, *Quantum Field Theory and the Standard Model* — primary
- **Srednicki**, *Quantum Field Theory* — free alternative
- **Weinberg** vols 1–3 — depth reference
- **Faddeev & Slavnov**, *Gauge Fields* — Soviet supplement
- **Polyakov**, *Gauge Fields and Strings* — Soviet supplement

### GR

- **Hartle**, *Gravity* — gentlest intro
- **Carroll**, *Spacetime and Geometry* — primary
- **Wald**, *General Relativity* — depth follow-on
- **MTW**, *Gravitation* — encyclopedic reference

### EFT

- **Manohar**, *Effective Field Theories* — lecture notes
- **Cohen**, TASI 2018 — modern EFT lectures
- **Burgess**, *Introduction to Effective Field Theory*

### Condensed matter

- **Altland & Simons**, *Condensed Matter Field Theory*
- **Coleman**, *Introduction to Many-Body Physics*
